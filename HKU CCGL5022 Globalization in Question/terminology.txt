privatization(私有化)
Homogeneity(同质化): 全球文化趋同（如麦当劳化、西方化, 全球产品标准化传播 | World Culture Theory / Westernization / McDonaldization, global spread of standardized products
Heterogeneity(异质化), 地方文化抵抗；强调差异与独特性；可能出现文化冲突 | Cultural resistance; emphasis on differences; potential cultural clashes
Hybridization(混杂化): “全球本土化”（Glocalization）, 全球理念被本地重新诠释, 融合创新 | Glocalization, global ideas reinterpreted locally; fusion of elements (e.g., Chifa, Nikkei)
Cultural conservatives(文化保守派): 存在“正宗”文化, 原创者最懂；创新不如传承 | Authentic culture exists, creators know best; innovation inferior to tradition
Cultural liberal(文化自由派): 文化服务于生活（吃、玩等）；应灵活适应；守旧无意义 | Culture serves practical purposes, adaptation > rigid preservation
economic capital(经济资本): 金钱、物质财富 | Economic: money, possessions
cultural capital(文化资本): 对主流文化习俗的“合法知识”，通过教育或实践获得；可储存在物品中 | Cultural: “legitimate knowledge” of customs (Chan, 2002); acquired via education/participation; stored in objects
social capital(社会资本): 人际关系与社群网络 | Social: relationships between people/communities
symbolic capital(象征资本): 声望、荣誉等无形资产 | Symbolic: intangible prestige and honour
constructivism (建构主义): 全球化由文化互动塑造；观念结构 > 物质结构 | Globalization shaped by cultural processes; ideas > material forces (Theys, 2018)
marxist/dependency theory (马克思主义/依附理论): 资产阶级全球剥削工人；核心-边缘剥削关系 | Bourgeoisie exploit global labor; core exploits periphery (Marx)
liberalism (自由主义): 主张放松贸易限制，促进经济文化交流 | Free trade → cultural/economic exchange (Riano, 2019)
world systems theory (世界体系理论): 核心国家剥削边缘地区资源与政治（Wallerstein, 1970s） | Core states exploit periphery (Kardulias, 2008)
network society (网络社会): 权力分散于互联网络（Castells, 1998） | Power decentralized across digital networks
world polity theory (世界政体理论): 全球趋同为单一文化 | World becoming homogenous (McNeely, 2012)
global village (全球村): 麦克卢汉的乌托邦隐喻：人类因技术天然互联 | Utopian metaphor: innate global interconnectedness (McLuhan & Powers, 1992)
ghost/specter (幽灵/ specter): 全球化不可知、无形；常用于媒体研究 | Globalization as unknowable, spectral (Wilson, 2003)
smallpox (天花): 隐喻：文化共存如疫苗接种，最终消除风险 | Cultural coexistence = vaccination → eradication of threat (Suvakovic et al., 2014)
plague (瘟疫): 福柯式隐喻：隔离“外来者”，维护同质性 | Quarantine foreign influence to preserve purity (Foucault, 1995)
